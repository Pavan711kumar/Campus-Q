CampusQ

Smart Campus Queue Optimization

Canteen Pre-Ordering System

The Problem

  • ⏳ Long waiting times in college canteens
  • 🏃 Students rushing between classes
  • 💵 Cash handling issues & order confusion

Our Solution

  • 📱 Pre-order food from anywhere on campus
  • 🕒 Choose a specific pickup time
  • 💳 Pay online securely
  • 🚀 Skip the line entirely!

How it Works (Students)

  • 1. Browse Menu: View available items on your phone.
  • 2. Add to Cart: Select what you want to eat.
  • 3. Pay Online: Quick and secure checkout.
  • 4. Pick Up: Grab your food at the chosen time.

How it Works (Canteen)

  • 📊 Dashboard: View incoming orders in real-time.
  • 👨‍🍳 Prepare: Accept and cook orders efficiently.
  • ✅ Notify: Mark orders as "Ready" for pickup.

Technology Stack

  • ⚛️ Frontend: React + Vite & Tailwind CSS
  • 🐍 Backend: Python FastAPI
  • 🔥 Database: Firebase Auth & Firestore
  • 💳 Payments: Razorpay Integration

Why CampusQ?

  • Saves precious time for students
  • Increases sales & efficiency for canteens
  • Modernizes the campus experience
